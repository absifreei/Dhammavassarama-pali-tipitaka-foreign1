内凡 [py] nei4fan2 [wg] nei-fan [ko] naebôm [ja] ナイボン naibon ||| The inner of higher ranks of ordinary disciples, as contrasted to waifan 外凡, lower, or "outer" ranks. In Hiinayaana, it is considered to be the stage of the Four Roots of Goodness sishangenwei 四善根位. In Mahaayaana, the three stages of the worthies sanxianwei 三賢位. [Credit] acm(entry) cw(py)
