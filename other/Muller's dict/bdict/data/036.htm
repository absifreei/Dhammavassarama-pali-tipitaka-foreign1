多羅 [py] duo1luo2 [wg] t'o-lo [ko] tara [ja] タラ tara ||| (1) An abbreviation for the translation of paatra (鉢多羅), a flat silver bowl placed in front of the Buddha image at ceremonies for incense, etc. (2) A transliteration of taala, a kind of tree. See 多羅樹 (tuoluoshou). (3) A transliteration of taaraa, the pupil of the eye. (4) An abbreviation of the transliteration of xiuduoluo 修多羅, Sanskrit suutra. (5) In the Esoteric sect, a transliteration for taarani, a woman bodhisattva. [Credit] acm(entry) cw(py)

多羅樹 [py] duo1luo2shu4 [wg] t'o-lo-shu [ko] tarasu [ja] タラス taraju ||| A transliteration of the Sanskrit taala. Also written in Chinese gaosongshou 高竦樹. A tree resembling the palm, which reaches a height of approximately 25 meters. Its flowers are white and its fruit is red, resembling a pomegranate. Its leaves are big enough to use as fans, and were also used for writing on. [Credit] acm(entry) ps(py)
