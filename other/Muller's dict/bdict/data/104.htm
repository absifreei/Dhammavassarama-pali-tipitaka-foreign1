癡 [py] chi1 [wg] ch'i [ko] ch'i [ja] チ chi ||| (moha, muudha): 'delusion', 'ignorance.' Also written yuchi 愚癡. The modern character is 痴. The defilement of confusion regarding all relative and absolute truths. In the Abhidharmako`sa-bhaasya it is listed as one of the evil producing elements; in the doctrine of the Yogaacaara school, it is listed as one of the primary defilement elements (六煩惱). Also, one of the three "roots of non-virtue" (三不善根) and one of the "ten secondary dullnesses" (十隨眠). A synonym for 無明. Regarded as the origin of all defilements. [Credit] acm(entry) cw(py)
