老子化胡經 [py] Lao3zi3 hua4hu2 jing1 [wg] Lao-tzu hua-hu ching [ko] Noja hwaho kyông [ja] ロウシケコキョウ Roushi keko kyou ||| Laozi Huahu jing (Sutra on the Conversion of the Barbarians); By Wang Fu 王浮. This text claimed that after his disappearance, Laozi went to India, converted people there, and became the Buddha. An early Chinese apocryphal sutra, written by a Taoist for the purpose of discrediting Buddhism and elevating Taoism. Texts such as this which were usually discovered by early Buddhist canonical catalogers and eventually rejected. [Dictionary References] iwa843 [Credit] acm(entry)
