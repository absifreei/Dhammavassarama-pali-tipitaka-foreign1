釋 [py] shi4 [wg] shih [ko] sôk [ja] シャク shaku ||| (1) To explain, to interpret. (2) A commentary that explains the meaning of a sutra or a `saastra (nirde`sa). (5) An abbreviation of 釋加 (Skt. `Saakya, Pali: Saakiya), a general surname applied to all East Asian monks and nuns during the earlier periods of the assimilation of Buddhism into East Asia, a custom probably initiated by Daoan 道安 of the Eastern Qin.
